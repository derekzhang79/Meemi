Meemi is a free client for the Meemi social network. The easiest way to share and collect your texts, your quotes, your images, your favourites links, to organize your events with your friends or collect your favourites video. It's totally free, simple and fast and allows you to meet new friends, follow their presence on in the "background noise" of every day, discovering their tastes and their personality through their shares.
